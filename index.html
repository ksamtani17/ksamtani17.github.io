About Me

A 360° marketer with luxury at heart

I'm a driven, multicultural marketing leader with a passion for luxury, fashion, and fragrance. Having lived and worked across India, Spain, and France, I bring an instinctively international mindset — I don't just understand global markets, I've lived inside them.

What sets me apart is the 360° breadth of my marketing experience. Across every role I've held, I have touched every dimension of the discipline — brand strategy, content creation, SEO, influencer marketing, e-commerce, data analytics, campaign management, visual merchandising, and cross-functional team leadership.

My immersion in the fragrance and luxury world began at Bon Parfumeur, where I shaped the brand's digital identity. At Globe Groupe, I stepped into the high-stakes world of Travel Retail across EMEA — leading campaigns, building Power BI dashboards, and coordinating international teams for measurable impact.

I am highly adaptable and a rapid learner — I consistently hit the ground running, absorbing environments at speed and excelling well ahead of the curve. Currently progressing in French (B1) as part of my full commitment to the Parisian professional world.
